Our Campus

The campus is laid aesthetiall with well constructed blocks to house the different departments. the Science Block, the HomeScience Block, the MBA Block, the Arts Block & P.G.Block are spacious and well maintained to accommodate classrooms,labooratories, seminar halls, multimedia centers, e-cassrooms and other facilities..

Campus Environment

The main Campus has an open and porous space. It has been a challenge to make it safe and secure for the community, particularly for women students.... All the measures, over the past years, have made the campus a much safer place, for women.

Campus Lifestyle

It's a diverse community of students at our college and the activities on offer are as diverse and varied too. There's never a dull moment on campus, as the activities on offer are as diverse and varied too - from entertainment to extra-curricular pursuits.

Student Affairs

All activities related to extracurricular programs, forums, clubs, community service, and entrepreneurship cell and students council are managed by the student affairs department. Counselors are available to guide students and share personal issues

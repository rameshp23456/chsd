History of the college –(1953-2014)

Our History

Ch.S.D.St.Theresa’s College is managed by the congregation of the sisters of St.Ann.The Congregation was founded by a noble couple Marquise Carlo Tancredi and Marchionous Gulia of Borolo,Italy.The desire to share God’s love with the poor and underprivileged children in any part of the world led the sisters to undertake the long and tedious journey to India.Mother Enrichetta Dominici the then poor and underprivileged children in any part of the world led the sisters to undertake the long and tedious journey to India.Mother Enrichetta Dominici.The then Mother General sent six Italian sisters to India,in 1871.

The sisters mission in Eluru,West Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh began in 1938 with establishing St.Theresa’s girls high school.The college was founded in 1953.

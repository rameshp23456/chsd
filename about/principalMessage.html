Principal Speaks...










Hearty Welcome to St.Theresa's College an autonomous premier women's college offering excellent education and empowering the rural women for the past 6 decades.

"Thamasoma Jyothirgamaya" - Lord lead us from darkness to light .With this prayer and motto the Theresians banish the darkness of ignorance,illiteracy,inequality and injustice;usher in a new dawn in the social and intellectual horizon of our students lives brighten their future with skill and self confidence and enflame their desire to become better and keep growing,achieving and succeeding for themselves,their families and for the society.

Since its humble beginning in 1953 the college has grown in leaps and bounds ,achieving laurels ,emerged as a trend setter in women's education .Autonomous for the past 27 years having earned the coveted College for Potential for Excellence (CPE) status from UGC in 2006,and a five star from NAAC in 1998 and from then A grade in II and III cycles,the institution teaches the young girls to give colour to their dreams ,power to their aspirations finding fulfilment,we equip them to emerge empowered women ,socially responsible citizens who will make a difference wherever they are and whatever they do.

As a Christian minority institution we give importance to truth ,Love and secularism,and train our students in the path of gospel values set down by Jesus Christ the teacher par excellence!

Hearty Welcome to St.Theresa's!!
“Let your Light Shine “
Dr.Sr.Mercy

Indira Gandhi National Open University ( IGNOU )

CH. S. D. St. Theresa's college for women became partner institute of Indira gandhi National Open Univeristy ( IGNOU) in April 2008. Under convergence Scheme. IGNOU is the largest university in enrollment on the planet. World Health Organisation recognised it as one of the top ten universities in the world in its standards.

The Programmes offered under convergence scheme are :

  • 1. Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.)
  • 2. Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • 3. M. A (English)
  • 4. M. A.(Hindi)
  • 5. M.Sc (Dietetics and Food Service Management)
  • 6. Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C. A.)
  • 7. P.G.Diploma in Human Resources Management (PGDHRM)
  • 8. P.G. Diploma in Financial Management (PGDFM)
  • 9. P. G. Diploma in Marketing Management (PGDMM)
  • 10. Certificate Course in Functional English (CFE)
  • 11. Certificate Course in Business Skills (CBS)
  • 12. Certificate Course in Information Technology (CIT)

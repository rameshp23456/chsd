Bank Of St.Theresa Students Savings Bank

The bank was reopened for the academic year 2013 – 14 on 18th June 2013. The bank accepts three kinds of deposits, fixed, Savings deposite and Daily deposits. This year the bank was able to collect Rs 2, 36, 370 deposits under these heads and lend around 2 lakhs loans. This year bank gave loans to students to buy cycles.

Commerce Department Library

To impart managerial skills and to inculcate reading habit among students the Commerce Department Library was established in 1999 with 40 books donated by lacturers and some students. Today there are around 800 commerce books, 200 general study books 150 fiction and 500 projects and other books.

Best.COM Career Master, Monthly Magazine

Commerce Dept published monthly magazine to impart managerial and marketing skills to B.Com students and to provide information on competetive exams and career guidance. Till date 3 issues were released and the 4th one will come in to the market in January.

The magazine is designed to cater to the needs and demends of those who are appearing for different kinds of competetive examinations. It covers G.K, Arthmetic reasoning, basic mathematics and statistical problems, english, current information, career guidance, abbrevations etc. The book received appreciation and good response from students.

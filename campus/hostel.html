Campus Hostels



An Integral part of the college are the hostels in the two campuses. Facilities are available for about 750 students who are under supervision of the wardens and assistant wardens. The resident staff also help them to feel at home.

Hostel Environment



The spirit of oneness is fortnightly as students come from different social, economic and religious backgrounds. The fort nightly, common recreation and celebration of Hostel day and other major festivals offer the students an opportunity to discover and develop their talents.

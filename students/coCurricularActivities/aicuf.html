All India Catholic University Federation(AICUF) All India Catholic University Federation(AICUF) is an All India Catholc Student Movement.It is affiliated to the International movement of Catholic students (IMCS),pax Romana.It has its National Office in Chennai.The AICUF members draw their inspiration from the divine person of Jesus Christ.They work with the motto to help in the growth of society organizing spiritual renewal programs,participating in social issues and finding solution,offering service to the needy in times of calamities and disaster are the main areas of activities the AICUFers are involved in.

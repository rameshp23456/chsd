Sports:

The theresians manifested their usual high standards and winning spirit .Their acheivements have been manifold and they made us proud by winning in many events bringing laurels to the college each time they went out to participate in inter collegiate games and sports meet.Our intermediate students continue to reign as champions for the 15th consecutive year.The junior intercollegiate games was organized at our junior campus.It is highly encouraging to work with highly disciplined and motivated band of students who keep improving their personal record and vie with each other to keep the theresian flag flying high.Kudos to our physical education department headed by Major Dr.Celine Rose.

Tutor Ward System: (C/O Ward Tutor):

The tutor ward system is one of the best practices of the College, which has been in vogue since 1971. Each ward tutor is entrusted with a group of 25 - 30 students. She caters to the academic intellectual, psychological and emotional needs of the ward students and maintains their progress reports. She also acts as a liaison between the student - parent - principal.

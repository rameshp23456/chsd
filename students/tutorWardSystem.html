Tutor Ward System: (C/O Ward Tutor):



Ward tutor /Mentoring system is a three decade old practice at St.Theresa’s.Every staff is assigned 25-30 students who will be their friend ,confidante and counsellor.The ward tutor maintains a record of academic,co-curricular achievements/progress of the wards ,interacts with the wards at least once a week helps in their growth and well being and intervenes when necessary to correct them and guide them.The practice builds good rapport between staff and students at a personal level.

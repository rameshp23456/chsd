Counseling Cell:

Counseling Cell has six members of the College and functions from 4.15 p.m. to 5.30 p.m. on weekdays. The students are free to contact the counselors, even other times with prior appointment. Dr.Nirmala Devi, the Campus Physicion, visits the College on Fridays from 12 p.m. to 1 p.m. and conducts medical check up to those students who are in need of it.

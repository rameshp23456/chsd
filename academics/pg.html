Post graduate program:

The post graduate courses are affiliated to Andhra University,Vishakhapatnam.Admissions take place through counselling based on AUCET and ICET ranking.

Academic year consists of 2 semesters with 30:70 internal and external marks weightage.Project work is compulsory except in MA English and Economics.



Courses Offered:
MSc-Mathematics
Msc - Organic Chemistry
Msc - Physics
Msc - MicroBiology
MA - English
MA - Economics

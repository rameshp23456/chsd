Degree Programmes:

St.Theresa’s UG programme were made autonomous from the year 1987.It was affiliated to Andhra University till 2014.Currently it is affiliated to the newly formed AdiKavi Nannaya University,Rajahmundry.Choice based credit system and innovative teaching learning and evaluation methods helps in the all round development of the students.ICT enabled teaching learning process,continuous assessment ,creative assignments ,makes learning interesting.

Project work in the final year gives orientation to research .Paper presentations ,participation in state and national level seminars or conferences,internships and hands on experience makes learning more complete.

The college follows Semester system of evaluation with 50:50 weightage for continuous assessment and semester end examination.

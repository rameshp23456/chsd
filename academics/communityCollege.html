Community College

The Community College system is established to afford easy access to the underprivileged students who are interested to directly enter into workforce after short term high touch remedial,career oriented,skill training programme.Multiple entry and exit are possible through the community college.Two Semester diploma program with 60 credits is being offered .

  • 1.ITeS (Information Technology Enabled Services)
  • 2.Fashion Designing

Special Features:

  • Soft Skill Training
  • IT Skill Training
  • Communication Skill
  • Campus Placement

Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent courses
Duration: 1 year (2 semesters)
